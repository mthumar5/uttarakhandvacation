    Delhi-Corbett (01N)-Nainital (02N)-Delhi

    Itinerary

    Day 01: Delhi-Corbett (250 km || Approx 07 Hours)

    On Arrival at Delhi pickup from Railway station / Airport and to Corbett. On arrival, check-in to the hotel. After dinner, sleep comfortably at the hotel for the night.

    Day 02: Corbett – Nainital ( 60 km approx 02 hours )

    Early morning go for a pre book safari (At extra Cost ) of the Jim Corbett National park, home to a large variety of flora and fauna. The famous royal Bengal tiger has its home here and you are sure to catch a glimpse or two of the Hunter of all hunters. After breakfast check out from the hotel and proceed towards Nainital . After reaching there check in hotel and spend the night in leisure .

    Day 03:- Nainital (Local Visit )

    After breakfast, proceed for the sightseeing tour, beginning with boating at the famous Naini Lake and a visit to the holy Naina Devi Temple. After that, visit famous picnic spots like Naina Peak, Snow View, Lands End etc enjoy day at leisure Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

    Day 04: Nainital-Delhi (280 km || Approx 07 Hours)

    Morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Delhi. Upon arrival at Delhi transfer to Airport/Railway station. For onward destination.
